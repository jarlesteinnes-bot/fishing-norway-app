Privacy Policy

Fishing in Norway App

Last Updated: September 20, 2024

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how "Fishing in Norway" ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • What: Approximate location coordinates when you use location-based features
  • Purpose: To show nearby fishing spots and provide location-specific weather information
  • Storage: Location data is not stored permanently and is only used during app sessions
  • Permission: Requires "Location When In Use" permission

Photos and Camera Access

  • What: Photos you take within the app for your catch log
  • Purpose: To document and store your fishing catches
  • Storage: Photos are stored locally on your device only
  • Permission: Requires camera and photo library access permissions

Data We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect personal information like name, email, or contact details
  • We do not track your identity across other apps or websites
  • We do not collect fishing location history or patterns
  • We do not access photos outside of the app's catch log feature

How We Use Information

  • Location Services: Only to display nearby fishing spots and provide accurate weather forecasts
  • Photos: Only for your personal catch log stored locally on your device
  • App Usage: We do not track or analyze how you use the app

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties

  • Weather data is retrieved from public weather services but your location is not shared with them
  • All photos remain on your device
  • No fishing activity or location data is shared

Data Security

  • All photos are stored locally using iOS security features
  • Location access is limited to when the app is in use
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • We follow iOS security best practices

Your Rights and Choices

Location Permission

  • You can disable location services for the app in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  • The app will still function but won't show nearby spots or location-based weather

Photo Access

  • You can revoke photo/camera permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security
  • This will prevent adding new catches to your log but won't affect existing photos

Data Deletion

  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data including photos
  • Clear app data through iOS Settings > General > iPhone Storage

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

Weather Data

  • The app uses weather APIs to provide fishing conditions
  • Weather services may have their own privacy policies
  • We do not share your location with weather services - only request data for general areas

Apple Services

  • The app uses standard iOS features (Camera, Location, Photo Library)
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policies and iOS permissions system

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of changes.
