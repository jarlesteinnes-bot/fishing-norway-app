Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I add my own fishing spots?
Use the "Add Spot" feature in the map view. Tap the + button and mark your location. You can add details like fish species, difficulty level, and notes.
‚ùì Why does the app need location permissions?
Location access allows us to show nearby fishing spots and provide accurate weather forecasts for your area. Your location data stays on your device and is never shared.
‚ùì Can I use the app offline?
Yes! All fishing spot information is available offline. Only weather data and initial content loading require an internet connection.

‚ùì Are the fishing regulations up to date?
